Cost Planning, Project Budget Development; and construction programming

  • Provision of a comprehensive Construction Cost Plan.
  • The ability to assist with budget formulation enables clients to reconcile desired outcomes with the cost reality
    of delivering a project.
  • The cost plan provides detail of a project's final costs including all trade pricing, preliminaries, consultant fees and Renascent's fees.
  • Once quotations have been received for a trade, Renascent would complete an assessment of quotations
    and prepare a recommendation for approval by the client.
  • The quotations and recommendations would be used to build a GMP budget, which once approved would form the basis of the contract sum.

As a single point of contact we are able to deliver projects from concept to handover.

Our responsibilities will include:

  • Single point of contact throughout the project, from concept to handover. We are able to act as both your
    Project Manager and Construction Manager; and accept full responsibility for the key aspects of project delivery,
    including: Cost, Time, Quality and providing a turn key solution.
  • Management of the design brief; thereby ensuring the brief is in line with budget and company expectations.
    • Procurement of appropriate project consultants, including tendering pricing, selection and ultimate
      appointment of Architectural consultants; Services engineer; Structural engineer; Building surveyor; and Acoustic and AV consultants (if required).
    • Managing the consultant team, thereby ensuring that timelines are adhered to, and documentation is
      co-ordinated.
  • Working with the selected designer in the preparation and lodgement of authority applications.
  • Checking authority's requirements for BCA compliance for egress, facilities, etc.
  • Preparation and monitoring of a Project Program that we accept full responsibility in achieving.

CONSTRUCTION MANAGER FOR COMMERCIAL FIT OUT, RETRO-FITTING, REFURBISHMENT, AND
CONSTRUCTION

Our responsibilities will include:

  • Tendering trade packages. Renascent takes full responsibility for the performance of every sub-contractor and supplier who is engaged to undertake the works.
  • Managing the site and every aspect of the construction process by providing experienced and suitable
    resources.
  • Liaising with building management and statutory authorities.
  • Conducting weekly site meetings with updated meeting minutes, programs and cost plans circulated to all
    relevant stakeholders.
  • Nil variations. We ensure that no sub-contractor variations are entertained unless a change to the scope is
    driven by the customer.
  • Inspecting defects and rectification of all defective works.
  • Obtaining all relevant statutory certificates to enable handover and ultimate occupation by the tenant.
  • Compilation and distribution of maintenance manuals to relevant stakeholders.
